NMBR1

Animation | Chararacter Design

Overview

NMBR1 is an animated music video originally completed in a group setting over 10 weeks for Simon Fraser University’s Animation class.

Roles: 3D Modelling | Character Designer

Timeline: Spring 2025

Tools: Blender | Figma | Photoshop

Goal:
Create a high-energy, bright, and fun music video that demonstrates the learned principles of animation as well as other concepts from the course.

Concepts

Once the team had selected a song to animate, we settled on a plot - an intense street race - and I sketched some rough concepts for characters which fit the aesthetic and narrative, and potential shots that matched the lyrics of the song.

Creating our Characters

After character designs were finalized, I moved on to modelling the character I designed in Blender. To do this, I came up with animations testing the model, which had the added benefit of helping me get comfortable with animating in Blender.
