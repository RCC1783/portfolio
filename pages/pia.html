Pia.

Games | Games | <Project Type>

Overview

Designed in a group over a semester, Pia is a highly developed concept for a mobile game that incorporates theories of human cognition and computer interaction, allowing players to better interface with and test their memory.

Roles: Gameplay Designer | Artist | Animator

Timeline: Spring 2024

Tools: Photoshop | Live 2D Cubism | Figma

Goal:
Create a game concept that incorporates theories of human cognition and computer interaction including but not limited to anomalous colour vision/colour blindness, affordances and signifiers, memory, and theories of multiple intelligence.
